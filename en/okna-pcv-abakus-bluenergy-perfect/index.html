bluENERGY PERFECT Windows in Białystok — economical and warm

bluENERGY PERFECT is the economical twin of the popular bluENERGY windows, on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 profile. A 6-chamber profile, Uw from 0,8 W/m²K and a choice of double or triple glazing — the perfect balance between price and insulation.

bluENERGY PERFECT — key parameters

bluENERGY PERFECT is the twin solution of the popular bluENERGY windows in an economical version, on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 profile. 6-chamber frame and sash profiles, 82 mm deep, give Uw from 0,8 W/m²K for the 1230/1480 mm reference window, and you choose the glazing unit yourself: 2 panes (Ug 1,1) or 3 panes (Ug 0,5).

It is a perfect balance: an economical choice and high insulation in one window. If you are after a good price without giving up warmth, bluENERGY PERFECT hits the sweet spot.

Why is bluENERGY PERFECT an economical choice?

bluENERGY PERFECT belongs to the PERFECT line — economical twins of proven windows. The savings come from a simpler yet still solid construction, not from poorer materials.

  • Two outer seals — colour-matched (black or grey), welded at the corners; the PERFECT line has no centre seal.
  • Choice of glazing unit — 2 panes (Ug 1,1) for budget or 3 panes (Ug 0,5) for maximum warmth.
  • Aluminium handle — durable and neat, typical of the PERFECT variants.

Unlike the premium bluSYNEGO (7-chamber frame, three seals, Roto Line Secustik handle), PERFECT focuses on a sensible price-to-spec ratio. Compare the lines in our range of PVC windows and pick the variant for your budget.

Reinforcement, hardware and security

Economical does not mean weak — bluENERGY PERFECT has burglar-resistance class RC2 and a solid load-bearing construction. The profiles are stiffened by 2 mm steel reinforcement and additional ribs that keep the window geometry stable for years.

  • Roto NX hardware — secure, smooth opening and the option to add security features.
  • Warm steel spacer bar — reduces the thermal bridge and the risk of condensation at the edge of the glass.
  • IRMC Production® welding technology — strong, neat profile corners.

Standard colours include Nebraska 33, Złoty dąb 51, Antracyt 55 and Antracyt Satynowy 88, while the semi-standard range adds Silver Grey 42, Woodec Oak 91 and Jet Black Matt 18 — plus many additional finishes to order.

Frequently asked questions

What makes the bluENERGY PERFECT line stand out?
bluENERGY PERFECT is the economical twin of the popular bluENERGY windows on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 profile. It has 6-chamber profiles, 82 mm depth and Uw from 0,8 W/m²K, and you choose the glazing yourself — 2 panes (Ug 1,1) or 3 panes (Ug 0,5). It offers a perfect balance of price and insulation.
How does bluENERGY PERFECT differ from bluENERGY?
bluENERGY PERFECT is the economical twin of the bluENERGY line: it has two outer seals (without an extra centre seal) and an aluminium handle, which makes it cheaper while keeping Uw from 0,8 W/m²K on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 profile. The glazing can be chosen as double or triple.
How many panes do bluENERGY PERFECT windows have?
bluENERGY PERFECT windows are available with double glazing (Ug 1,1 W/m²K) or triple glazing (Ug 0,5 W/m²K). The triple-glazed unit gives maximum warmth, while the double-glazed one is a more economical solution.
Do bluENERGY PERFECT windows have a centre seal?
No. bluENERGY PERFECT belongs to the PERFECT line, which has two colour-matched outer seals (black or grey) welded at the corners, without an additional centre seal. A centre seal is found in premium versions such as bluSYNEGO.
Is there a warranty and instalments on bluENERGY PERFECT windows?
Yes. Pro-Plast gives a 5-year warranty on bluENERGY PERFECT windows, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. We install with our own crew using warm, airtight fitting, provide transport, and the measure-up and quote are free.
