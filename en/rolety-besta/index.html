BESTA — A Cassette with Two Guide Rails

BESTA is a fabric roller blind in a cassette with two guide rails. Unlike blinds in which the fabric runs right against the glass, BESTA does not overlap the glazed area — and once the blind is rolled up it becomes practically invisible. It is a blind you only notice when you are using it.

The two guide rails steer the fabric evenly on both sides, so the blind runs steadily even in windows that have shifted a little over the years. That makes BESTA a natural choice for blind replacements and older buildings, where you want a discreet, neat window finish.

BESTA Blind Colours

You can match the cassette and aluminium rails of BESTA to the colour of the window and the interior. Three colours are available as standard, and at an extra charge the whole blind can be made in a shade from the RAL palette.

  • Standard colours — white, silver and brown.
  • At an extra charge — colours from the RAL palette, to match the colour of your joinery or facade.

The RAL palette gives plenty of freedom when the blind has to work perfectly with an unusual window or interior colour. During the survey we will show you fabric samples and swatches, so you can choose the shade on the spot, with no guesswork.

Who Is BESTA For?

BESTA is the choice for those who want a discreet blind — invisible when you are not using it — and fitted without intruding on the glazed area. It works particularly well when replacing blinds and in older windows.

  • Older buildings and replacements — a blind recommended directly for these uses.
  • Invisible when rolled up — when the blind is raised, it practically disappears from view.
  • Does not overlap the glazed area — you keep the full window opening when the blind is rolled up.
  • Stable guidance — two guide rails hold the fabric evenly on both sides.

If you are fitting a blind in a new window and prefer the fabric to run right against the glass, consider the FINEZJA version. Would you rather control the light with slats instead of shading? Take a look at venetian blinds. Book a free survey in Białystok and the surrounding area — we will advise whether BESTA suits your windows.

Frequently asked questions

What makes the BESTA blind stand out?
BESTA is a fabric interior roller blind in a cassette with two guide rails. It does not overlap the glazed area, and when rolled up it is practically invisible. This makes it discreet and recommended above all for blind replacements and for older buildings.
How does BESTA differ from the FINEZJA blind?
FINEZJA has one guide rail and fabric that runs right against the glass — it overlaps the glazed area and works well in new windows. BESTA has two guide rails, does not overlap the glazed area and is practically invisible when rolled up, which is why we recommend it for older buildings and for replacements. During the survey we will advise which one suits your windows better.
What colours does the BESTA blind come in?
As standard, the cassette and aluminium rails are available in white, silver and brown. At an extra charge the whole blind can be made in any shade from the RAL palette, which lets you match it to an unusual window or interior colour.
Is BESTA suitable for old windows?
Yes. BESTA is recommended directly for older buildings and for blind replacements. The two guide rails steer the fabric steadily on both sides, while the cassette does not overlap the glazed area and stays practically invisible when rolled up — which is why it looks good even with older joinery.
Do you fit BESTA blinds in Białystok?
Yes. We carry out the survey and fitting of blinds with our own Pro-Plast crew in Białystok and the surrounding area. The on-site survey and the quote are free, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. Book a visit via the contact page — we will advise on choosing the right blind for your windows.
