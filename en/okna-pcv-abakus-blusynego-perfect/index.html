bluSYNEGO PERFECT Windows in Białystok — Synego 80 in an economical version

bluSYNEGO PERFECT is the economical variant of ABAKUS windows on the REHAU Synego 80 AD profile. A 6-chamber profile, Uw from 0,82 W/m²K and a choice of double or triple glazing give good insulation at a sensible price — with the renowned REHAU system behind it.

bluSYNEGO PERFECT — key parameters

bluSYNEGO PERFECT is the economical variant on the renowned REHAU Synego 80 AD profile. 6-chamber frame and sash profiles, 80 mm deep, give Uw from 0,82 W/m²K for the 1230/1480 mm reference window with triple glazing, and you choose the unit yourself: 2 panes (Ug 1,1) or 3 panes (Ug 0,5).

It is a sensible compromise: the renowned REHAU Synego system and solid insulation at a price within reach of most budgets. A good choice when you want the profile brand without paying for the premium version.

How does bluSYNEGO PERFECT differ from bluSYNEGO?

bluSYNEGO PERFECT is the economical twin of the premium bluSYNEGO — the same REHAU Synego system, but in a simpler, cheaper configuration.

  • 6 chambers instead of 7 — frame and sash profiles in the economical version, still on the Synego 80 AD platform.
  • Two outer seals — colour-matched (black or grey), welded at the corners; the PERFECT line has no centre seal.
  • Aluminium handle — durable and neat, typical of the PERFECT variants.
  • Choice of glazing unit — 2 panes (Ug 1,1) for budget or 3 panes (Ug 0,5) for maximum warmth.

The premium bluSYNEGO has a 7-chamber frame, three seals (with a centre seal), a Roto Line Secustik handle and Uw from 0,79. PERFECT is the same profile brand at a more accessible price. Compare both variants in our range of PVC windows.

Reinforcement, hardware and security

bluSYNEGO PERFECT is rated to burglar-resistance class RC2, so the economical configuration is no compromise on security. The profile is stiffened by steel reinforcement in the open version (closed optionally), which keeps the window geometry stable for years.

  • Roto NX hardware — secure, smooth opening and the option to add security features.
  • Warm steel spacer bar — reduces the thermal bridge and the risk of condensation at the edge of the glass.
  • IRMC Production® welding technology — strong, neat corners of the REHAU Synego profile.

Standard colours include Winchester 1208L, Złoty Dąb 7512, Antracyt 4443, Antracyt Gładki 930L and Midnight Black Matowy 1980L, while the semi-standard range and made-to-order add over 30 further finishes.

Frequently asked questions

What makes the bluSYNEGO PERFECT line stand out?
bluSYNEGO PERFECT is the economical variant of ABAKUS windows on the renowned REHAU Synego 80 AD profile. It has 6-chamber profiles, 80 mm depth and Uw from 0,82 W/m²K with triple glazing, and you choose the glazing yourself — 2 panes (Ug 1,1) or 3 panes (Ug 0,5).
How does bluSYNEGO PERFECT differ from bluSYNEGO?
bluSYNEGO PERFECT is the economical twin of the premium bluSYNEGO: it has a 6-chamber profile (instead of a 7-chamber frame), two outer seals without a centre seal and an aluminium handle, with Uw from 0,82 W/m²K. The premium bluSYNEGO has three seals, a Roto Line Secustik handle and Uw from 0,79 W/m²K.
How many panes do bluSYNEGO PERFECT windows have?
bluSYNEGO PERFECT windows are available with double glazing (Ug 1,1 W/m²K) or triple glazing (Ug 0,5 W/m²K). The triple-glazed unit gives Uw from 0,82 W/m²K and maximum warmth, while the double-glazed one is a more economical solution.
Do bluSYNEGO PERFECT windows have a centre seal?
No. bluSYNEGO PERFECT belongs to the PERFECT line, which has two colour-matched outer seals (black or grey) welded at the corners, without an additional centre seal. The centre seal is found in the premium bluSYNEGO version.
Is there a warranty and instalments on bluSYNEGO PERFECT windows?
Yes. Pro-Plast gives a 5-year warranty on bluSYNEGO PERFECT windows, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. We install with our own crew using warm, airtight fitting, provide transport, and the measure-up and quote are free.
