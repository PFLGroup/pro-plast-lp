CLASICTHERM Windows in Białystok — the Economy uPVC Line

CLASICTHERM is our entry-level uPVC window option for those on a tighter budget. A 6-chamber profile, a 70 mm frame and a flexible choice of glazing unit — from Uw 0,94 W/m²K to the economy version at Uw 1,4 — let you match the window to your needs and costs.

CLASICTHERM — Key Specs

CLASICTHERM is the economy uPVC window line for those who want to replace their joinery on a tighter budget and not overpay for figures they won't use. A 6-chamber profile with a 70 mm frame and a 70 or 80 mm sash gives a solid base, while the warmth is decided by the chosen glazing unit.

Flexible Glazing Unit — Two Versions

The biggest advantage of CLASICTHERM is the flexible choice of glazing unit — you decide how much you want to invest in insulation. Two versions are available, and they translate directly into the warmth of the whole window.

  • 40 mm unit, Ug 0,6 W/m²K → Uw = 0,94 W/m²K — the warmer version, a good compromise of price and insulation for living spaces.
  • 24 mm unit, Ug 1,1 W/m²K → Uw = 1,4 W/m²K — the most economical version, sensible for utility rooms and wherever cost matters above all.

This way one line covers the whole house — in the rooms you fit the warmer 40 mm unit, and in the garage, boiler room or utility room the cheaper 24 mm unit. If maximum warmth matters to you, compare CLASICTHERM with the warmer uPVC windows in our range.

Who Is the CLASICTHERM Line For?

CLASICTHERM is a budget choice, but a considered one. In Białystok and the surrounding area it often works well where many windows have to be replaced at once and the total cost matters.

  • A tighter budget — sensible insulation without paying extra for passive-class figures.
  • Utility rooms — garages, boiler rooms, spaces where price matters above all.
  • Whole houses on a smaller budget — in the version with the 40 mm unit (Uw 0,94) for living spaces.
  • Rental investments — a reasonable ratio of price to quality.

MACO MULTI-MATIC hardware with a wind-resistant catch gives comfortable operation, and there are over 30 veneer designs to choose from, so even the entry-level option needn't be a dull white window. During the survey we'll help match the unit to each room.

Frequently asked questions

What Uw rating do CLASICTHERM windows have?
CLASICTHERM has a flexible unit, so the Uw depends on the chosen version. With the 40 mm unit (Ug 0,6 W/m²K) the window reaches Uw = 0,94 W/m²K, and with the economy 24 mm unit (Ug 1,1) — Uw = 1,4 W/m²K. We recommend the warmer version for rooms and the cheaper one for utility rooms.
How do the two glazing units in CLASICTHERM differ?
The 40 mm unit at Ug 0,6 W/m²K gives a warmer window (Uw 0,94) and suits living spaces. The 24 mm unit at Ug 1,1 W/m²K is cheaper and gives Uw 1,4 — sensible for garages, boiler rooms or utility rooms, where cost matters above all.
Who is the CLASICTHERM line for?
CLASICTHERM is the entry-level option for those on a tighter budget. It works well in utility rooms, and in the version with the 40 mm unit also in whole houses where the budget is smaller. It is still far warmer than the old joinery it usually replaces.
What hardware and colours does CLASICTHERM have?
CLASICTHERM has MACO MULTI-MATIC hardware with a wind-resistant catch, which holds the tilted sash steady in the wind. There are over 30 veneer designs to choose from, so even the entry-level option needn't be an ordinary white window.
Can CLASICTHERM windows be bought on instalments?
Yes. The purchase of CLASICTHERM windows can be spread over instalments, which is often the deciding factor on a tighter budget. In addition we give a 5-year warranty, and we carry out the fitting with our own Pro-Plast crew. The on-site survey and the written quote are free.
