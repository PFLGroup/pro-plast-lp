Warm Windows That Pay for Themselves in the Podlasie Climate

In Białystok the winter lasts a long time and can turn genuinely bitter. Every weak point in the building envelope means a higher heating bill. That's why, when it comes to windows, it doesn't pay to look at the purchase price alone – what really counts is how much money you keep in the house over the heating seasons that follow.

What's behind our energy efficiency

Our uPVC windows are built on six-chamber profiles and triple-glazed units with Ug = 0.5 W/m²K. In practice that means less heat escaping through the glass and the frame. The figure for the whole window starts as low as Uw 0.78 W/m²K – an excellent result for a home that's meant to deliver real savings.

Window lines and their parameters

We'll match the window line to your budget and your expectations. Our range includes, among others:

  • PERFECTHERM – Uw from 0.78 W/m²K, our choice for those most demanding when it comes to warmth.
  • redENERGY – Uw 0.80 W/m²K, a strong balance of energy efficiency and price.
  • MODERNTHERM – Uw 0.81 W/m²K, a good window for most houses and flats.
  • CLASICTHERM – Uw 0.94 W/m²K, a sensible choice when a keen price is your priority.
  • bluEnergy – another line from our range, specified individually to suit the project.

We'll work out which profile is best for you during the survey and a chat about how you heat your home and what you expect from your windows.
