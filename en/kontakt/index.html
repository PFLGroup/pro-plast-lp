Get in touch and book your free window survey

Planning to replace your windows or doors in Białystok? Drop us a line or give us a call – we'll come over, take the measurements and prepare a quote at no cost and with no obligation.

Free measurement & quote

The easiest way is just to talk

Replacing your windows starts with a few simple questions. How many windows do you have? What size are they? Are you after warmer glazing to see you through a Podlasie winter, or is keeping out the street noise more of a priority? You don't need to have all the answers straight away – that's exactly what getting in touch with us is for, so we can talk it all through at your own pace.

The quickest way to sort things out is over the phone – in a few minutes we'll agree on the scope of work and a convenient time for the survey. Prefer to write? Send us an email with a short description of what you need, and if you have them, add a few photos or rough dimensions. We'll reply with specifics, not vague generalities.

We cover Białystok and the surrounding towns and villages across Podlasie. If you're not sure whether we travel to your address, just ask. More often than not, the answer is yes.

How to book a free survey

An on-site survey is standard practice with us, not an optional paid extra. We turn up with a tape measure and plenty of experience, because accurate measurements are what later decide whether a window fits the opening like a glove and whether there'll be any draughts around the edges. The whole process comes down to three steps.

Step 1 – contact and initial chat

You call or write and tell us what you'd like replaced and where. At this stage we'll point out what to look out for and which window lines make sense for your needs – from warmer uPVC windows to aluminium frames.

Step 2 – a survey at your home

We agree on a convenient time and come to the address you've given us. We'll measure the openings, check the condition of the reveals and advise on windowsills, blinds or insect screens. It's the perfect moment to ask all your questions in person.

Step 3 – a no-obligation quote

Based on the survey, we put together a precise quote. No charges and no pressure – the decision is entirely yours, in your own time. If you decide to go ahead, we arrange a date for installation and delivery, and where needed, the option to spread the cost over instalments.

Want to skip the reading and get straight to it? Book your free survey – one phone call is all it takes.

Where to find us

Our office sits in the centre of Białystok, on ulica Św. Rocha. It's one of the city's main thoroughfares, so getting here is easy whether you're coming from the roundabout or from the city centre. By public transport, several bus routes stop nearby.

If you're driving, head towards the centre – Św. Rocha runs close to the square with the distinctive church that's hard to miss. You'll find parking spaces in the area, although the very heart of the city can get busy at peak times, so it's worth allowing a few extra minutes to park.

You'll find our exact address, phone numbers and email in the page footer and on our contact card. If you're planning to visit us in person, it's best to call ahead – that way we can make sure someone from the team is free to see you and give you their time without rushing.

Opening hours

Our sales office is open on weekdays. The exact hours are:

  • Mon–Fri: 9:00–16:00
  • Sat–Sun: closed

If none of these times work for you, just let us know – we can usually arrange an on-site survey to fit around your schedule. Outside office hours, the best option is to send an email, and we'll call you back or reply as soon as we can.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a window survey and quote cost?
Nothing. Both the on-site survey and the quote are free of charge and carry no obligation to buy. We come over, take the measurements and prepare an offer – then you decide in your own time. The price of the windows themselves depends on the number of units, their sizes and the line you choose, which is why we only give a reliable figure once the survey is done.
Do you install windows in winter, in the Podlasie frosts?
Yes. We carry out installations through the winter season too – we use materials and techniques suited to working in lower temperatures, and we replace a single window efficiently to keep the opening exposed for as short a time as possible. In the event of genuinely extreme frosts we'll agree the best timing with the customer, but the season itself is no obstacle.
