BLUENERGY Windows in Białystok — Energy-Efficient and Quiet

BLUENERGY is an energy-efficient, award-winning uPVC window line in a top-grade profile. A 6-chamber profile 82 mm deep, a twin-chamber (triple-glazed) unit at Ug 0,5 W/m²K and three gaskets — including an extra central one — give warmth and noticeably better sound insulation.

BLUENERGY — Key Specs

BLUENERGY is an energy-efficient uPVC window line made of top-grade uPVC, created with both warmth and quiet in mind. A 6-chamber profile with an 82 mm build depth combines with a twin-chamber (triple-glazed) unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K, and its exceptional tightness is decided by an arrangement of three gaskets.

The Central Gasket — Warmth and Quiet

What most sets BLUENERGY apart is its arrangement of three gaskets: two classic abutment gaskets and an extra central gasket. A standard window has two gaskets — the central one forms a third barrier between the frame and the sash.

  • Better sound insulation — the extra chamber between the gaskets clearly damps outside noise, which you'll appreciate by a busy street.
  • Greater tightness — the third barrier reduces draughts and helps keep the heat inside.
  • Less dust and fewer draughts — a tighter sash means a calmer microclimate in the room.

Combined with the triple-glazed unit at Ug 0,5 and the deep 82 mm profile, BLUENERGY is a window that copes well both with the cold of a Podlasie winter and with city noise. If you are comparing lines for quiet, set it against the rest of the uPVC windows in our range.

The „Jakość Roku 2015" Award — Who This Line Is For

BLUENERGY was honoured with the „Jakość Roku 2015" (Quality of the Year 2015) title — outside confirmation that it combines good specs with solid workmanship. It is a line for those who put energy efficiency and acoustic comfort first.

  • Houses and flats by busy streets — where the central gasket genuinely improves the quiet.
  • Investors who value energy efficiency — the triple-glazed unit at Ug 0,5 and the deep 82 mm profile.
  • Bedrooms and studies — rooms where quiet matters most.
  • Modern façades — the deep build gives a slim, attractive window.

If you want warmth and quiet at the same time, BLUENERGY is a strong candidate. During the survey we'll compare it with other lines and pick a solution to suit your needs.

An energy-efficient window delivers its full benefits only with a good fit — even the best line loses out if the insulation where it meets the wall is skipped. That is why we fit BLUENERGY with our own Pro-Plast crew, with a warm, airtight fit and a careful finish.

Frequently asked questions

What sets the BLUENERGY line apart?
BLUENERGY is an energy-efficient uPVC window line in a top-grade 6-chamber profile 82 mm deep, with a twin-chamber (triple-glazed) unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K. Its hallmark is an extra central gasket that improves warmth and sound insulation. The line was honoured with the „Jakość Roku 2015" (Quality of the Year 2015) title.
Why does BLUENERGY have an extra central gasket?
The central gasket is a third barrier between the frame and the sash, alongside the two standard abutment gaskets. The extra chamber between the gaskets clearly damps outside noise and increases the window's tightness, which translates into better sound insulation and fewer draughts.
What glazing unit does BLUENERGY have?
BLUENERGY has a twin-chamber, that is triple-glazed, unit with a Ug = 0,5 W/m²K. Combined with the deep 6-chamber 82 mm profile and the arrangement of three gaskets, this gives very good thermal and acoustic insulation.
Does BLUENERGY damp noise well?
Yes. BLUENERGY has good sound insulation thanks to the extra central gasket, which forms a further barrier to sound. It is a good choice for flats and houses by busy streets and for bedrooms and studies, where quiet matters.
What award did the BLUENERGY line receive?
The BLUENERGY line was honoured with the „Jakość Roku 2015" (Quality of the Year 2015) title. It is outside confirmation that it combines good energy-efficiency specs with solid workmanship — confirming the energy-efficient character and the quality of this uPVC window line.
