Roller (Cassette) Insect Screens in Białystok — No Removal for Winter

A roller insect screen hides the mesh in a cassette like a blind. You pull it out when you need it, and after the season it stays in place — nothing to take off or store. It is a convenient, durable all-year solution. We measure and fit in Białystok and the surrounding area.

How Does a Roller Insect Screen Work?

A roller insect screen has the mesh wound onto a roller hidden in a small cassette fitted above the window or beside the door. You pull it out when you want fresh air without insects, and when it is not needed the mesh rolls back into the cassette on its own, like a blind. Hence the second name: cassette insect screens.

It is a solution for windows and doors, made to measure for a specific opening. It runs smoothly and quietly, and the mesh tucked away in the cassette is protected from dust and weather. Among our insect screens, the roller type is dearer than the framed one but more convenient in everyday use.

An All-Year Solution — Nothing to Take Off

The greatest advantage of a roller insect screen is that there is no seasonal fuss. You do not need to take anything off or store it — after summer you simply roll the mesh into the cassette and leave it in place. All winter the mesh is tucked away and protected, so it does not work under snow and does not collect dirt.

This is especially convenient on hard-to-reach windows — for instance upstairs or over the sink — where putting a frame on and taking it off each season can be awkward. It also suits those who simply want to forget about the whole thing for years and have the screen on hand all year round.

Frequently asked questions

How does a roller insect screen differ from a framed one?
A roller insect screen has the mesh rolling into a cassette like a blind and stays in place all year — you take nothing off for winter. A framed insect screen is a taut mesh in a rigid frame that is removed and stored in autumn. The roller type is more convenient, the framed one cheaper.
Does a roller insect screen have to be removed for winter?
No. After the season you simply roll the mesh into the cassette and leave the screen in place. All winter the mesh is tucked away in the cassette and protected, so it does not work under snow or collect dirt. It is an all-year solution with no seasonal fuss.
Are roller insect screens fitted on doors?
Yes. We fit roller insect screens on windows and on balcony and terrace doors. Depending on the layout of the entrance, the mesh runs vertically or horizontally. We make every screen to measure and choose the right version during the survey.
Does a roller insect screen run quietly and smoothly?
Yes. The mesh rolls in and out smoothly and the guides run quietly, so the screen is convenient in everyday use. Tucked away in the cassette, the mesh is protected from dust and weather, which adds to the durability of the solution.
Who is a roller insect screen for?
It works best where you do not want to take anything off and store it — on hard-to-reach windows upstairs, over the sink or simply for those who prefer to have the screen on hand all year. It is a more convenient, though dearer, solution than the classic frame.
