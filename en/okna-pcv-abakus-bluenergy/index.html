ABAKUS bluENERGY Windows in Białystok — Premium with bluEvolution 82

bluENERGY is an award-winning Salamander uPVC window line on the bluEvolution 82 platform. Uw 0,76 W/m²K, a triple-glazed unit at Ug 0,5, eco-friendly passive-house-compliant production and Roto NX hardware rated up to 130 kg — premium for demanding projects.

bluENERGY — Key Specs

bluENERGY is a premium Salamander uPVC window for those unwilling to compromise on insulation. It is built on the bluEvolution 82 platform with an 82 mm build depth and 6-chamber frame and sash profiles, and reaches a reference-window value (1230/1480) of Uw = 0,76 W/m²K.

Warmth comes from a triple-glazed, twin-chamber unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K, a warm plastic spacer bar between the panes and an arrangement of three gaskets: two abutment gaskets and one central one, welded at the corners. This line was designed with passive construction in mind.

Why Did bluENERGY Win the „Jakość Roku" Award?

bluENERGY was honoured with the „Jakość Roku" (Quality of the Year) title — outside confirmation that it combines very good specs with solid workmanship. The award concerns a window designed from the start as a premium solution for energy-efficient homes.

  • Passive-house compliance — Uw 0,76 and the deep 82 mm profile work well in homes with low energy demand.
  • Eco-friendly production — a line made in an environmentally responsible way.
  • Three gaskets — two abutment and one central, welded at the corners, for warmth and quiet.
  • A warm spacer bar between the panes — reduces thermal bridges at the glass edge and the risk of condensation.

It is a line for investors who expect top quality in classic, clean styling. If you are comparing premium windows, set bluENERGY against the rest of the uPVC windows in our range.

Roto NX Hardware, Colours and Security

bluENERGY runs on Roto NX hardware rated up to 130 kg — this matters for large, heavy sashes, which on weaker hardware drop over time. A 2 mm steel reinforcement adds rigidity, and security is handled by RC2 class with a Roto Line Secustik anti-burglary handle.

  • Roto NX hardware up to 130 kg — sure guidance and closing even for large glazing.
  • RC2 class — makes it harder to lever the window open, a sensible standard for ground floors.
  • 9 standard colours: Nebraska 33, Złoty Dąb 51, Orzech 21, Mahoń 26, Dąb bagienny 25, Antracyt 55, Antracyt Satynowy 88, Jet Black Matt 18, Woodec Oak 91.
  • Plus semi-standard and decorative variants — you can easily match the window to the façade and the interior.

It is a set that suits modern homes in Podlasie with large glazing. During the survey we will pick the colour and the scope of security to suit your project.

Fitting in Białystok, Warranty and Instalments

A premium window at Uw 0,76 delivers its full benefits only with a good fit — skipped insulation where it meets the wall spoils the result despite the very best specs. That is why we fit bluENERGY with our own Pro-Plast crew, with a warm, airtight fit and a careful finish; we weld the profiles using Abakus IRMC Production technology.

We start with a free on-site survey and a free written quote, we provide transport, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. As a local Białystok firm we give a 5-year warranty and aftercare. Book a free survey and find out the bluENERGY window price for your home.

Frequently asked questions

What Uw value do bluENERGY windows have?
bluENERGY windows reach Uw = 0,76 W/m²K for the reference window 1230/1480. It is provided by the 6-chamber Salamander bluEvolution 82 profile with an 82 mm depth combined with a triple-glazed, twin-chamber unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K.
How does bluENERGY differ from goGRAIN?
Both lines are built on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 platform and have Uw = 0,76 W/m²K. bluENERGY is a classic premium line with the „Jakość Roku" (Quality of the Year) award, compliant with passive construction, while goGRAIN is a bio-hybrid with 10% spelt husk in the profile and lower CO₂ emissions.
What award did the bluENERGY line receive?
The bluENERGY line was honoured with the „Jakość Roku" (Quality of the Year) title. It is outside confirmation that it combines very good energy-efficiency specs with solid workmanship — the window was designed as a premium solution for energy-efficient homes.
What load capacity does the hardware in bluENERGY windows have?
bluENERGY runs on Roto NX hardware rated up to 130 kg. This matters for large, heavy sashes, which on weaker hardware drop over time. A 2 mm steel reinforcement adds rigidity, and security is provided by RC2 class with a Roto Line Secustik handle.
What colours are bluENERGY windows available in?
bluENERGY is available in 9 standard colours: Nebraska 33, Złoty Dąb 51, Orzech 21, Mahoń 26, Dąb bagienny 25, Antracyt 55, Antracyt Satynowy 88, Jet Black Matt 18 and Woodec Oak 91, plus semi-standard and decorative variants.
