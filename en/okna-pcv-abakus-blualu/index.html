ABAKUS bluALU PVC Windows with an Aluminium Cap

bluALU combines a durable, designer aluminium face with the warmth of a PVC profile. A deeper 94 mm build, Uw 0,76 W/m²K and the RAL palette of the cap — with installation by Pro-Plast in Białystok.

What is the bluALU aluminium cap?

bluALU are PVC windows with a powder-coated aluminium cap on the profile — that is its main distinguishing feature. You get a durable, designer aluminium face on the outside and the warmth and energy efficiency of the PVC profile on the inside.

The aluminium cap increases the build depth to 94 mm — deeper than the other ABAKUS lines. The profile remains 6-chamber, with a 2 mm steel reinforcement and additional stiffening ribs.

What warmth and security does bluALU provide?

bluALU reaches Uw 0,76 W/m²K for the 1230/1480 reference window, with a triple-glazed, 2-chamber unit at Ug 0,5 W/m²K. The construction complies with the passive house standard.

Security is provided by Roto NX hardware and the Roto Line Secustik handle, and the windows meet the RC2 class. A warm edge spacer between the panes limits heat loss at the edge of the glass.

  • Triple-glazed, 2-chamber unit, Ug 0,5 W/m²K
  • Compliance with the passive house standard
  • Roto NX hardware, Roto Line Secustik handle
  • 2 mm steel reinforcement and additional ribs

Which RAL colours does the bluALU cap come in?

In bluALU the colour is the colour of the aluminium cap from the RAL palette, not the PVC foil — which is why the face is uniform, durable and weather-resistant. This gives a precise, designer façade look.

Selected aluminium cap colours include, among others:

  • White (R07.2), Silver (R01.1)
  • Matt Titanium (R01.3), Matt Black
  • Old Gold (R05.5), Grey-Brown (R04.1)

You will find the full range in the PVC windows section; if you are planning a cohesive finish for the window zone, we will also match the window sills. We will settle the RAL colour via contact with the office in Białystok.
