KS SYSTEM Doors — Sectional, Double-Leaf and Industrial

At Pro-Plast we rely on KS SYSTEM doors — a manufacturer that builds door curtains from monolithic panels with a constant thickness of 40 mm, insulated with PUR foam of a density of at least 38 kg/m³. It is a thick, warm and quiet structure that, in the Podlasie climate, genuinely limits heat loss from a garage adjoining the house. Every KS SYSTEM door meets stringent standards for resistance to water penetration, wind load and air permeability.

We divide the range into four groups, so that we can match the right solution to each garage and building. Sectional garage doors are the most common choice for single-family homes — the curtain lifts up to the ceiling and nothing protrudes beyond the outline of the garage (see sectional garage doors). Double-leaf doors are classic side-hinged doors for garages and outbuildings (see double-leaf doors). For halls, workshops and business premises we offer industrial doors with reinforced parameters.

Garage Door Automation — SOMFY and SOMMER

Almost every customer today chooses an electric drive — you pull up to the garage, press the remote and do not have to get out of the car. For garage doors we offer SOMFY automation from the DEXXO range: a model for smaller doors, a stronger one for intensive use and a smart variant with app control (see garage door automation). SOMFY drives come with a 5-year warranty, integrated obstacle detection and an anti-burglary lock.

For industrial doors we choose SOMMER drives and controllers — prepared for heavy loads and continuous operation (see industrial automation). Whatever the type of door, we will recommend the drive that suits its size and weight, not the most expensive one in the catalogue.

Survey and Fitting in Białystok and the Surrounding Area

A well-working door starts with a thorough survey. We come to you, check the width and height of the opening, the depth of the garage, the height of the lintel, the space under the ceiling and the way the services are routed. Only on this basis do we determine the type of door, the colour, the panel structure and the drive. The survey and the quote are free.

The fitting is carried out by our crew — the same specialists who fit windows and doors, so they know the realities of Białystok homes, detached garages and semi-detached houses. We offer transport and purchase by instalments. For details and a survey appointment get in touch or call: 602 488 413 or 85 746 14 46.

Frequently asked questions

What garage doors does Pro-Plast offer in Białystok?
We offer KS SYSTEM doors in three groups: sectional garage doors (the most common choice for homes), double-leaf doors (side-hinged, for garages and outbuildings) and industrial sectional doors for halls and business premises. Plus SOMFY automation (garage) and SOMMER (industrial). We measure and fit everything with our own crew.
What sets KS SYSTEM doors apart?
The curtain of KS SYSTEM doors is made of monolithic panels with a constant thickness of 40 mm, insulated with PUR foam of a density of at least 38 kg/m³. The sheet metal is double-sided — galvanised Z100 steel on the inside, Z225 with a thickness of 0.48 mm and a polyester coating on the outside. This structure is warm, quiet and resistant to the weather.
What automation do you choose for the doors?
For garage doors we offer SOMFY DEXXO drives (OPTIMO, PRO 1000 and the smart SMART IO) with io-homecontrol control and the TaHoma app, obstacle detection and a 5-year warranty. For industrial doors we choose the SOMMER GIGAsedo drive with the GIGAcontrol controller, prepared for heavy loads and continuous operation.
Are the door survey and quote free?
Yes. The on-site survey and the quote are free. We come to Białystok and the surrounding area, check the opening, the lintel and the space under the ceiling, and then choose the type of door, the colour, the panel structure and the drive. We give the exact figure after inspecting the opening, and the purchase can be spread over instalments.
