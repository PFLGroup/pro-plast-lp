PERFECTHERM Windows in Białystok — the Warmest uPVC Line

PERFECTHERM is our premium uPVC window line with a Uw from 0,78 W/m²K. A class A profile, 6-chamber, with an 80 mm build depth and a 48 mm unit at Ug 0,5 — for energy-efficient homes and low heating bills.

PERFECTHERM — Key Specs

PERFECTHERM is the warmest uPVC window line in the Pro-Plast range and the natural choice when you want to cut heat loss to the absolute minimum. A class A profile, 6-chamber, with a deep 80 mm build and a thick triple-glazed unit, it reaches a Uw from 0,78 W/m²K — a top-shelf figure on the window market.

The deep 80 mm build in the frame and sash makes room for a wide 48 mm unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K, while the 6 chambers in the profile improve the insulation of the frame itself, not just the glass. As a result the whole window — not only its best part — holds the heat exactly as the label states.

Who Is the PERFECTHERM Line For?

PERFECTHERM makes sense wherever every kilowatt-hour counts. In Białystok and across the Podlasie region the heating season drags on for months, so the warmest window pays back the difference in price fastest through a lower bill.

  • Energy-efficient and low-energy homes — when you design a building for low heat demand, Uw 0,78 W/m²K fits those assumptions.
  • Replacing old joinery — you feel the difference in comfort and in the bill from the very first winter.
  • Large glazing on the cold side — north, east, spots exposed to wind, where an ordinary window can „pull” cold in.
  • An investment for years — when you don't want to come back to the subject of windows at the next rise in energy prices.

If you are building or renovating with low bills in mind, this is the line for you. If the budget is tight, we'll point you to cheaper options — compare all the uPVC windows in our range.

Hardware, Colours and Veneers

It is the hardware that decides a window's everyday comfort. PERFECTHERM runs on MACO MULTI-MATIC hardware with snap catches and lift devices, which make closing even large, heavy sashes easier and let you use the tilt-and-turn function smoothly.

You choose the looks separately from the insulation. The profile core colour comes in white, anthracite, brown and caramel, so the window looks consistent in the reveal too. On top of that we offer over 30 veneer designs — from wood-effect to plain colours — so you can match the window to the façade and the interior independently.

It's worth settling the colour together with the sills and roller blinds. Once we've matched your windows, we'll suggest coordinating extras in a matching palette, so the whole façade works together.

Fitting, Warranty and Instalments

The warmest window, badly fitted, stops being the warmest — poor foam or skipped insulation where it meets the wall can undo the good figures on the label. That is why we fit PERFECTHERM with our own Pro-Plast crew, with a warm, airtight fit and a careful finish.

We start with a free on-site survey and a free written quote, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. As a local Białystok firm we provide aftercare and a 5-year warranty — if an adjustment is needed, we are right on hand. Book a free survey and find out the exact PERFECTHERM price for your home.

Frequently asked questions

What Uw rating do PERFECTHERM windows have?
PERFECTHERM windows reach a Uw from 0,78 W/m²K — the warmest uPVC window line in the Pro-Plast range. This low figure is delivered by a class A, 6-chamber profile with an 80 mm build depth combined with a 48 mm unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K.
What glazing unit does PERFECTHERM have?
PERFECTHERM has a glazing unit 48 mm thick with a Ug = 0,5 W/m²K. It is a thick sealed unit, allowed by the 80 mm build depth of the frame and sash — which is why the whole line is so warm.
What hardware do you use in PERFECTHERM windows?
In PERFECTHERM windows we fit MACO MULTI-MATIC hardware with snap catches and lift devices. The lift devices make closing large, heavy sashes easier, and the whole system gives comfortable, durable operation of the tilt-and-turn function.
In what colours are PERFECTHERM windows available?
The PERFECTHERM profile core colour comes in white, anthracite, brown and caramel. In addition we offer over 30 veneer designs, on one side or both — from wood-effect to plain colours, matched to the façade and the interior.
Who do you recommend the PERFECTHERM line for?
We recommend PERFECTHERM for energy-efficient homes and wherever low heating bills matter. It works well when replacing old joinery and with large glazing on the cold side. It is an investment for years, the warmest line in our range.
Is there a warranty and instalments on PERFECTHERM windows?
Yes. We provide a 5-year warranty on PERFECTHERM windows, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. We carry out the fitting with our own Pro-Plast crew with a warm, airtight fit, and as a local Białystok firm we provide aftercare. The survey and quote are free.
