MB-45 System in Białystok — Light Aluminium Without a Thermal Break

MB-45 is an aluminium system without a thermal break, for uses where thermal insulation is not key. A depth of 45 mm in windows and 54 mm in doors gives a slim, rigid structure for internal partitions and shopfronts.

MB-45 — Key Specs

MB-45 is an aluminium system without a thermal break — without an insulating insert in the profile. The lack of a thermal break is a deliberate choice: where there is no warm-cold boundary, insulation is unnecessary, and what counts is slimness, rigidity and appearance. That is why MB-45 goes into internal partitions and shopfronts, and into light structures.

The build depth is 45 mm in windows and 54 mm in doors. With no thermal break the system is slim and light, while the profiles keep the rigidity of aluminium. The system is sealed with EPDM gaskets, resistant to UV and ageing.

When Is Thermal Insulation Not Needed?

The first thing worth settling with aluminium is whether the profile separates warm from cold. If it does — a building entrance, the shopfront of a heated unit — you need a warm system with a thermal break. If it does not — a partition inside the building, a wall within a single temperature zone — insulation is unnecessary, and paying for a thermal break makes no sense.

It is exactly for this second situation that MB-45 was created. It works well where a similar temperature prevails on both sides of the profile, so all the attention goes to strength and looks, not to warmth. Where the profile does have to separate warm from cold, we choose the warmer MB-60 system with a thermal break, which we describe together with the rest of our aluminium joinery.

Who Is the MB-45 System For?

MB-45 is a system for structures where thermal insulation is not key and what counts is rigid, slim and durable aluminium. It works well in premises and buildings in Białystok and the surrounding area.

  • Internal partitions and shopfronts — dividing walls and glazing within a single temperature zone.
  • Doors in unheated rooms — where heat does not escape outside the building.
  • Light aluminium structures — where a low profile weight and a clean look matter.
  • Smart, rigid builds — when you need the strength of aluminium without the cost of a thermal break.

If the structure is to be external and separate warm from cold, MB-45 will not be the right choice — then we reach for a system with a thermal break. We will advise which aluminium system suits your project. Compare the whole range on the aluminium joinery page.

Frequently asked questions

Does the MB-45 system have a thermal break?
No. MB-45 is an aluminium system without a thermal break. It is a deliberate choice for uses where thermal insulation is not key — internal partitions and shopfronts, doors in unheated rooms and light structures. Where the profile separates warm from cold, a system with a thermal break is chosen.
What build depth does the MB-45 system have?
The build depth of the MB-45 system is 45 mm in windows and 54 mm in doors. With no thermal break the system is slim and light, while the profiles keep the rigidity and durability of aluminium.
What is the MB-45 system suitable for?
MB-45 is suitable for uses where thermal insulation is not key: internal partitions and shopfronts, doors in unheated rooms and light aluminium structures. It works well where a similar temperature prevails on both sides of the profile and what counts is rigidity and appearance.
How does MB-45 differ from MB-60?
MB-45 has no thermal break (depth 45/54 mm) and is used for internal partitions and shopfronts, where insulation is not key. MB-60 has a thermal break (60/69 mm) and is a warm external system — for windows, doors, shopfronts and conservatories. The choice depends on whether the profile separates warm from cold.
What gaskets does the MB-45 system have?
The MB-45 system is sealed with EPDM gaskets. EPDM resists UV and ageing, holds its seal well over the years and does not harden over time, which translates into durability for the partition, shopfront or door, also in Podlasie conditions.
