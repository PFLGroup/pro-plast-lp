Aluminium Horizontal Blinds in Białystok

Classic blinds with horizontal aluminium slats — available in 16 and 25 mm tapes, with chain control and a mounting that doesn't damage the window profile. The on-site survey and quote are free.

Horizontal blinds — key specs

Horizontal blinds are a classic that simply works — tilting the aluminium slats lets you smoothly adjust how much light comes in and the angle it falls at, from full daylight to almost complete shading of the room. Thanks to aluminium they are lightweight, durable and moisture-resistant, so they prove their worth in the kitchen and bathroom too.

You match the tape to the size of the window: 16 mm looks more discreet on small sashes, while 25 mm sits more calmly on large glazed areas. You adjust the blinds on two axes — you set the slat angle and the height they're lowered to.

Chain mechanism and fitting that doesn't damage the profile

You operate horizontal blinds with an integrated chain mechanism — in one movement you raise and lower the slats and set their angle. It's a proven, quiet and reliable solution, with no power supply and no configuration.

The most important thing about the fitting is that the concealed mounting system doesn't damage the window profile. We fit the blinds on the sash, so they work whether or not the window is tilted open, and the warranty on your joinery stays intact.

Who horizontal blinds are for and how we fit them in Białystok

Horizontal blinds are the most common choice for single windows in a flat or house — anywhere a simple, neat form and precise light control matter. Aluminium slats feel at home in rooms exposed to moisture, which is why we're happy to fit them in the kitchen, bathroom or utility room.

We work locally in Białystok and the surrounding area. We start with a free on-site survey and a free written quote, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. Once you accept, we order the made-to-measure blinds and fit them with our own crew — the fitting involves no wet work, so we carry it out in winter too. If you're after other shading, take a look at our other blinds, as well as roller shutters and insect screens — we'll match the whole thing in one visit.

Frequently asked questions

What tape widths are horizontal blinds available in?
We offer horizontal blinds with tapes in widths of 16 mm and 25 mm. The narrower 16 mm tape looks more discreet on small windows, while the wider 25 mm sits more calmly on large glazed areas. The slats are aluminium, and control is by an integrated chain mechanism.
Does fitting horizontal blinds damage the window profile?
No. We fix horizontal blinds with a concealed mounting system whose fitting doesn't damage the window profile. We fit them on the sash, so they work regardless of whether the window is open, and the warranty on your joinery stays intact.
How do you control horizontal blinds?
Horizontal blinds are controlled by an integrated chain mechanism. In one movement you raise and lower the slats and adjust their angle, setting both the height and the amount of light let in. The mechanism needs no power supply and no configuration.
What windows do horizontal blinds fit?
Horizontal blinds are compatible with windows made of uPVC, aluminium and wood. We fit them on the sash, and the concealed mounting system doesn't damage the profile, so they suit most windows in flats and houses in Białystok and the surrounding area.
Are horizontal blinds suitable for the kitchen and bathroom?
Yes. Aluminium slats are lightweight, durable and moisture-resistant, so horizontal blinds work well in the kitchen, bathroom and utility room. They're easy to wipe down and keep clean, and damp air doesn't warp them.
