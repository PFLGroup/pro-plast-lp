ABAKUS bluDECORO Industrial Windows — without external sills

bluDECORO are industrial windows without external sills built on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 platform. Passive, warm and energy-efficient — with a free measurement and installation by Pro-Plast in Białystok.

What are bluDECORO industrial windows?

bluDECORO is an ABAKUS industrial window line designed for installation without external sills — that is its key distinguishing feature. A clean façade face with no external ledge supports cohesive, modern frontages of homes and buildings.

The windows are built on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 platform: 6-chamber frame and sash profiles, an 82 mm build depth, three gaskets (2 stop gaskets and 1 central) welded at the corners and a 2 mm steel reinforcement.

Are bluDECORO windows passive and warm?

Yes — bluDECORO are passive, warm and energy-efficient windows. They reach Uw 0,76 W/m²K with a triple-glazed, 2-chamber unit at Ug 0,5 W/m²K, which genuinely lowers heating bills in the Podlasie climate.

Security is provided by Roto NX hardware and the Roto Line Secustik handle, and the windows meet the RC2 class. A warm edge spacer between the panes limits thermal bridges at the edge of the glass.

  • Triple-glazed, 2-chamber unit, Ug 0,5 W/m²K
  • 2 mm steel reinforcement
  • Roto NX hardware, Roto Line Secustik handle
  • RC2 burglar resistance class

What colours and decors does bluDECORO offer?

bluDECORO has the widest decorative palette among the ABAKUS lines — from standards to rich wood decors, matte colours, greens, blues and reds. This lets you match the window to any façade.

Available finishes include, among others:

  • Standard: Nebraska 33, Złoty Dąb 51, Orzech 21, Mahoń 26, Dąb bagienny 25, Antracyt 55, Antracyt Satynowy 88
  • Semi-standard: Silver Grey 42
  • Wood decors, matte colours, greens, blues and reds

You will find the other models in the PVC windows section, and we will match the colour during a conversation via contact.
