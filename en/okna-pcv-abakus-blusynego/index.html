bluSYNEGO Windows in Białystok — premium REHAU Synego 80

bluSYNEGO is the top ABAKUS window line on the REHAU Synego 80 profile. A 7-chamber frame, Uw from 0,79 W/m²K, a triple-glazed unit with Ug 0,5 and three seals (two outer + one centre) deliver warmth, quiet and a standard close to passive-house building.

bluSYNEGO — key parameters

bluSYNEGO is a premium PVC window line on the REHAU Synego 80 profile, built for the highest thermal and acoustic insulation. A 7-chamber frame and 6-chamber sash, 80 mm deep, combine with a triple-glazed unit of Ug = 0,5 W/m²K to give Uw from 0,79 W/m²K for the 1230/1480 mm reference window.

This line meets passive-house building standards and pairs very good thermal insulation with markedly better sound damping — a sound choice wherever you want warmth and quiet in one window.

How does bluSYNEGO differ from the PERFECT version?

bluSYNEGO is the premium variant — the richest in the whole Synego family. The key difference is the 7-chamber frame and the three-seal system: two outer seals plus an additional centre seal welded at the corners.

  • Better acoustic insulation — the extra chamber between the seals damps outside noise, which you will appreciate on a busy street in Białystok.
  • Passive standard — Uw from 0,79 W/m²K and Ug 0,5 put this line at the level of energy-efficient construction.
  • Roto Line Secustik handle — with the SECUSTIC locking mechanism that makes it harder to shift the hardware from outside.

The PERFECT versions (bluSYNEGO PERFECT, bluENERGY PERFECT) are economical twins: a 6-chamber profile, two outer seals without a centre seal and an aluminium handle. bluSYNEGO without the PERFECT suffix is the richest option. See our full range of PVC windows to compare the lines.

Security, hardware and durability

bluSYNEGO offers some of the highest strength figures in its class. The window is rated to burglar-resistance class RC2 and fitted with Roto NX hardware with a load capacity up to 130 kg and anti-pry catches.

  • Steel-reinforced frames — rigidity and geometry held for years, without warping.
  • Roto Line Secustik handle — the SECUSTIC locking mechanism further hinders levering the sash open.
  • Resistance to heavy rain and strong wind — proven in the demanding Podlasie climate.
  • HDF formula — a unique PVC recipe that resists dirt and is easy to keep clean.

The profile is joined with Abakus IRMC Production® welding technology for strong, neat corners. Nine colours are available as standard (including Winchester, Złoty Dąb, Antracyt and Czarny Midnight Black Matowy), plus over 25 finishes to order.

Frequently asked questions

What makes the bluSYNEGO line stand out?
bluSYNEGO is a premium ABAKUS window on the REHAU Synego 80 profile: a 7-chamber frame, 80 mm depth, a triple-glazed unit of Ug = 0,5 W/m²K and Uw from 0,79 W/m²K. It has a three-seal system (two outer + one centre), Roto NX hardware, a Roto Line Secustik handle and RC2 class. It meets passive-house building standards.
How does bluSYNEGO differ from bluSYNEGO PERFECT?
bluSYNEGO is the premium version with a 7-chamber frame, three seals (including a centre seal) and a Roto Line Secustik handle, with Uw from 0,79 W/m²K in a triple-glazed unit. bluSYNEGO PERFECT is the economical twin: a 6-chamber profile, two outer seals without a centre seal, an aluminium handle, Uw 0,82 and a choice of 2 or 3 panes.
What Uw value do bluSYNEGO windows have?
bluSYNEGO windows reach Uw from 0,79 W/m²K for the 1230/1480 mm reference window, with a triple-glazed unit of Ug = 0,5 W/m²K. That is a result on the level of passive-house building.
Are bluSYNEGO windows secure?
Yes. bluSYNEGO windows have burglar-resistance class RC2, Roto NX hardware with a load capacity up to 130 kg and anti-pry catches, and a Roto Line Secustik handle with the SECUSTIC locking mechanism. Steel-reinforced frames ensure high strength.
Is there a warranty and instalments on bluSYNEGO windows?
Yes. Pro-Plast gives a 5-year warranty on bluSYNEGO windows, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. We install with our own crew using warm, airtight fitting, provide transport, and the measure-up and quote are free.
