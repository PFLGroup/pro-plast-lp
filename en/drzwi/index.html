Doors in Białystok — from your front step to every room

We supply and fit exterior, interior and PVC doors. We'll advise you, measure up at your place and fit everything so there are no winter draughts and the key keeps turning smoothly for years.

Exterior doors

Your front door is the first thing a guest sees and the last barrier between the cold outside and the warmth indoors. Winters in Białystok can be brutal, so when choosing exterior doors we look at insulation first and good looks second. We'll show you models for a detached house, for a side terrace and for a flat — each built around a different priority.

Warm doors for the home

For homes we recommend doors with thermally insulated infill and a thermal break that separates the cold side of the leaf from the warm one. That stops water condensing on the threshold and keeps your heating from escaping through the entrance. It matters most in older houses around Białystok, where a draughty old door can chill the whole hallway.

Built-in security

We build security in layers: a reinforced frame, several locking points, anti-prise pins on the hinge side and a solid cylinder lock. We won't pretend any door is impossible to break through — what we will say honestly is that well-chosen anti-burglary doors in Białystok dramatically increase the time a break-in takes, and that's usually enough to put an intruder off. We'll recommend a resistance class that suits whether you live in a block of flats or a detached house on the outskirts.

Designs and finishes

We have smooth leaves in a modern style, models with glazing to bring light into the hallway, and versions with grooved detailing to match a traditional façade. Colours run from classic anthracite grey to warm wood-effect foils. If you're replacing your windows too, it's worth matching the colours — we'll show you the samples side by side so the whole thing comes together.

Interior doors

Inside the house, different things matter: keeping a room quiet, easy cleaning, and a leaf that won't start sticking after a few years. Good interior doors in Białystok are the ones that, after a year of use, still close as smoothly as they did on day one.

For rooms and bathrooms

For bedrooms and the living room we choose solid leaves that dampen sound. For the bathroom and kitchen — finishes that stand up to moisture and steam, with a ventilation undercut or an air-flow sleeve. We'll tell you plainly which ranges hold up in homes with children and which need gentler handling.

Sliding and pocket doors

When there's no room to swing a leaf open, we offer sliding solutions — wall-mounted or tucked into a cassette inside the wall. They're handy in narrow hallways and where a kitchen opens onto the living room. We'll tell you whether your wall layout even allows that kind of installation before you order anything.

Frames and handles

A door is more than the leaf — the frame matched to the wall thickness and the hardware matter just as much. We'll fit adjustable frames for uneven walls (typical in renovated flats) and handles that suit the style of the rest of your interior.

PVC doors

Where resistance to moisture and minimal upkeep are the priority, PVC doors are the answer. We make them from the same brand-name profiles as our windows, so they combine a tight seal with easy use.

For the terrace and balcony

Balcony and terrace doors in PVC are a natural extension of our window range. We can make them as a tilt-and-turn version or as large glazed units that flood the living room with light. The profiles can be 6-chamber, and with triple-glazed units they reach a low heat transfer coefficient — we'll match the details to the rest of the windows in your home.

For utility and back rooms

For the garage, boiler room, utility room or a garden-side entrance, PVC doors are practical: no painting required, easy to wipe down and good with moisture. They're a sensible choice where timber would quickly suffer and looks aren't the top concern.

Easy to look after

PVC doesn't need oiling or varnishing — just wipe it down with a damp cloth, and grease the seals and hardware once a season. We'll show you how to look after them yourself so they keep working without a hitch.

Choosing and fitting

The worst thing you can do is buy doors by guesswork and leave the fitting to a random crew. With us it's a single process from start to finish, and the experience gathered over hundreds of jobs in and around Białystok really shows when the reveals are tricky.

How it works, step by step

  1. Free on-site measurement — we come out, measure the opening and check the condition of the wall and threshold.
  2. Free quote — we present specific models and prices, with no hidden charges for the obvious.
  3. Order and delivery — we deliver the doors with our own transport.
  4. Professional fitting — we seat, level, seal and adjust the hardware.
  5. After-sales care — we stay on hand should anything need adjusting.

Fitting in winter

We fit doors in the winter season too — using foams and materials suited to low temperatures, and we try to carry out the swap quickly to keep the opening exposed for as little time as possible. If you have a particular deadline in mind before the holidays or the frosts set in, let us know early, as it's a busy time.

Got a question, or simply want a price for a replacement? Book a free measurement or give us a call — we'll advise you before you order a thing.

Frequently asked questions

How much do exterior doors cost fitted in Białystok?
The price depends on the model, its thermal and security class and the state of the opening, so we won't quote blind. The fairest approach is to book a free measurement — then you'll get a firm quote including delivery and fitting, with no charges for the obvious.
Do you fit doors in winter?
Yes. We work through the winter season too, using foams and materials suited to low temperatures. We aim to carry out the swap efficiently to keep the opening exposed for as little time as possible. On frosty days it's worth booking a date well in advance.
What guarantee do your doors come with?
We provide a guarantee on the products, and after fitting we stay on hand as part of our after-sales care — if the doors ever need their hardware adjusting, just get in touch. We'll give you the guarantee details for your specific model when we quote.
Can you replace the doors and windows at the same time?
Absolutely, and we often do — we combine jobs to match the colours and cut down on the number of crew visits. While we're measuring up the doors, we can take a look at the windows too if you're planning to replace them.
