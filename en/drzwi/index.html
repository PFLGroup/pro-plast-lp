What doors do we offer?

In one place you can choose doors for the whole house with us — from a large terrace glazing to leaves for individual rooms. Each type has a different priority, so we don't sell one solution for everything. We'll tell you what really works for you.

PVC doors — terrace and entrance

Where a tight seal, light and resistance to moisture matter, PVC doors are the answer. The strongest item is the HS terrace door (tilt-and-slide) on a Salamander profile: huge glazing, an effortless slide and a low step-free threshold. Plus side-hung balcony doors and PVC side entrance doors. You'll find the details and full specification on the PVC doors page.

Brand-name exterior doors

An entrance door is the last barrier between the frost and the warmth inside. Our range includes warm, secure exterior doors from established manufacturers — with thermal insulation, thermal thresholds and multi-point locks. We'll recommend a class and finish to suit the façade; more on the exterior doors page.

Brand-name interior doors

Inside, what counts is keeping a room quiet, easy upkeep and a leaf that still closes smoothly after years. We have brand-name interior doors in various types and finishes, with adjustable frames for uneven walls. You'll find an overview on the interior doors page.

Measurement, fitting, guarantee and instalments

The worst thing you can do is buy doors by guesswork and leave the fitting to a random crew. With us it's a single process from measurement to adjusting the hardware.

  1. Free on-site measurement — we come out, measure the opening and check the condition of the wall and threshold.
  2. Free quote — we present specific models and prices in writing, with no charges for the obvious.
  3. Delivery and professional fitting — we seat, level, seal and adjust.
  4. After-sales care — we stay on hand after fitting.

We fit brand-name profiles, give a 5-year guarantee, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. We fit doors in the winter season too, using foams and materials suited to low temperatures. Want a firm price, or simply a quote for a replacement? Book a free measurement — we'll advise you before you order a thing.

Frequently asked questions

What types of doors does Pro-Plast offer in Białystok?
We offer PVC doors (HS terrace, balcony and side entrance), brand-name exterior doors and brand-name interior doors. All with advice, a free measurement and fitting by our own crew. You'll find the details of each group on the PVC, exterior and interior door pages.
Are the door measurement and quote free?
Yes. The on-site measurement and quote are free and with no obligation. We come out, check the opening and the state of the wall and threshold, and present the quote in writing. Only then do you decide.
What guarantee do you give on doors?
We give a 5-year guarantee on doors, and after fitting we stay on hand as part of our after-sales care — if the hardware ever needs adjusting, just get in touch. We'll give you the guarantee details for your specific model when we quote.
Can the door purchase be paid in instalments?
Yes, the purchase of the doors together with fitting can be spread over instalments. It's most convenient to discuss this at the free measurement, once we know the scope of work and a firm price in writing.
Can you replace the doors and windows at the same time?
Absolutely, and we often do — we combine jobs to match the colours and cut down on the number of crew visits. While we're measuring up the doors, we can take a look at the windows too if you're planning to replace them.
