STAR System in Białystok — the Warmest Aluminium Joinery

STAR is our warmest aluminium window-and-door system. A 6-chamber profile with an 80 mm build depth and a frame K = 1,0 W/m²K combines warmth with large glazing — for homes and places where energy efficiency matters.

STAR — Key Specs

STAR is the warmest aluminium window-and-door system in the Pro-Plast range — we reach for it where aluminium is going into a heated interior and at the same time has to hold large glazing. It is a 6-chamber system with raised thermal insulation and a thermal break, which limits heat escaping through the profile.

The 80 mm build depth makes room for a thick sealed glazing unit — up to 24 mm — while the six chambers in the profile improve the insulation of the frame itself, not only the glass. As a result the heat transfer coefficient of the frame is K = 1,0 W/m²K: a very good figure for an aluminium structure.

Who Is the STAR System For?

STAR makes sense wherever you want large aluminium glazing but without losing warmth. In Białystok and across the Podlasie region the heating season drags on for months, so the warmest aluminium system pays back the difference in price fastest through a lower bill.

  • Detached and energy-efficient homes — when you want a warm aluminium window or door in a heated interior.
  • Large glazing in the living room — glass walls and panoramic windows that uPVC can no longer carry at those sizes.
  • A modern, slim look — a narrow aluminium frame lets in the maximum amount of light.
  • An investment for years — aluminium does not warp in the sun and, once powder-coated, does not fade.

If warmth and the price of an ordinary domestic window matter most to you, sometimes a good uPVC window will be the wiser choice. We choose STAR where aluminium gives the edge: large formats and durability. Compare all our aluminium systems on the aluminium joinery page.

Gaskets, Fitting and Service in Białystok

The warmest profile, badly fitted, stops being the warmest — poor foam or skipped insulation where it meets the wall can undo the good figure on the label. That is why we fit STAR with our own Pro-Plast crew, with a warm, airtight fit and a careful finish.

The system is sealed with EPDM gaskets, resistant to UV, frost and ageing — they do not harden through Podlasie winters and keep their seal for years. We start with a free on-site survey and a free written quote, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. As a local Białystok firm we provide aftercare and a 5-year warranty. Frequently asked questions

What heat transfer coefficient does the STAR system have?
The frame of the STAR system reaches a coefficient of K = 1,0 W/m²K — a very good figure for an aluminium structure. It is delivered by a 6-chamber profile with an 80 mm build depth and a thermal break, combined with a thick sealed glazing unit up to 24 mm. STAR is the warmest aluminium system in the Pro-Plast range.
What glass can be used in the STAR system?
The STAR system takes a sealed glazing unit up to 24 mm thick. This is allowed by the profile's 80 mm build depth. The thicker glass and the six chambers in the profile give a low frame heat transfer coefficient of K = 1,0 W/m²K.
Who do you recommend the STAR system for?
We recommend STAR for detached and energy-efficient homes and wherever warmth matters with large glazing. It is the warmest of our aluminium systems, so it works well in living rooms with glass walls and panoramic windows in heated interiors.
What gaskets does the STAR system have?
The STAR system is sealed with EPDM gaskets. EPDM resists UV, frost and ageing, holds its seal well over the years and does not harden through Podlasie winters, which translates into lasting insulation for the aluminium window or door.
Do you fit STAR windows and doors in Białystok with a warranty?
Yes. We carry out the fitting with our own Pro-Plast crew in Białystok and the surrounding area, with a warm, airtight fit. We provide a 5-year warranty and offer instalments, and the on-site survey and written quote are free and come with no obligation.
