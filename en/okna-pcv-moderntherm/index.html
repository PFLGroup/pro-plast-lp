MODERNTHERM Windows in Białystok — the All-Round uPVC Line

MODERNTHERM is a solid, all-round uPVC window line with a Uw of 0,81 W/m²K. A 6-chamber profile, an 80 mm build depth and a 48 mm unit at Ug 0,5 — a proven choice for houses and flats that takes everyday use without trouble.

MODERNTHERM — Key Specs

MODERNTHERM is our most all-round uPVC window line — a sensible middle ground between warmth and price that suits most houses and flats. A 6-chamber profile, an 80 mm build depth and a triple-glazed unit give a whole-window coefficient of Uw = 0,81 W/m²K, a figure very close to the warmest lines.

The frame and sash, 80 mm deep, hold a wide 48 mm unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K, while the 6 chambers improve the frame's insulation. It is a line designed to run trouble-free for years and to ask no compromises day to day.

Who Is the MODERNTHERM Line For?

MODERNTHERM is a good choice when you are after a solid, warm window without paying extra for passive-class figures. It is an „all-purpose” line — it works in a detached house and in a block-of-flats apartment alike.

  • Detached houses — all-round insulation at Uw 0,81 W/m²K for typical construction.
  • Flats and blocks — replacing windows with warmer, quieter ones without overpaying.
  • Investors who value durability — perimeter hardware and a 6-chamber profile mean strength for years.
  • When PERFECTHERM is too much — MODERNTHERM gives very similar warmth at a more reasonable price.

If you are hesitating between lines, compare the specs of all the uPVC windows in our range — and better still, book a survey, during which we'll advise the specific option for your building.

Hardware, Colours and Veneers

MODERNTHERM runs on perimeter MACO hardware with a full set of features that improve comfort and security: a wind-resistant catch holds the tilted sash steady in the wind, two anti-lift keeps make prising harder, and a lift device with a lock makes closing large sashes easier.

You choose the colour independently of the insulation. The profile core comes in white, anthracite, caramel and brown, and on top of that we offer over 30 veneer designs — wood-effect and plain. That way you can match the window to the façade and to the interior, on both sides too.

It's worth settling the window colour together with the sills and roller blinds, so the whole thing works together. Once we've matched the line, we'll suggest coordinating extras in a matching palette.

Fitting, Warranty and Instalments

A good window has to be set well — a frame set out of true or skipped insulation where it meets the wall can spoil the result despite solid figures. That is why we fit MODERNTHERM with our own Pro-Plast crew, with care for the seal and the finish.

We begin our cooperation with a free on-site survey and a free written quote, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. As a local Białystok firm we give a 5-year warranty and we are right on hand should anything need adjusting. Book a free survey and find out the MODERNTHERM price for your house or flat.

Frequently asked questions

What Uw rating do MODERNTHERM windows have?
MODERNTHERM windows reach a Uw = 0,81 W/m²K. It is a very good figure for an all-round line — delivered by a 6-chamber profile with an 80 mm build depth combined with a 48 mm unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K.
How does MODERNTHERM differ from PERFECTHERM?
Both lines are 6-chamber and have an 80 mm build depth and a 48 mm unit at Ug 0,5. MODERNTHERM has Uw = 0,81 W/m²K, while the warmest PERFECTHERM (class A) has Uw from 0,78. The difference is small, so MODERNTHERM is a good choice when you want similar warmth at a more reasonable price.
What hardware does the MODERNTHERM line have?
MODERNTHERM has perimeter MACO hardware with a wind-resistant catch, two anti-lift keeps and a lift device with a lock. The anti-lift keeps make prising the sash harder, and the lift device makes closing large windows easier.
In what colours are MODERNTHERM windows available?
The MODERNTHERM profile core comes in white, anthracite, caramel and brown. In addition we offer over 30 veneer designs — wood-effect and plain, on one side or both — so you can match the window to the façade and the interior.
What buildings does MODERNTHERM suit?
MODERNTHERM is an all-round line — it suits both detached houses and block-of-flats apartments. It is a good choice when you are after a solid, warm window for years without paying extra for passive-class figures. During the survey we'll advise the option for your specific building.
Is there a warranty and instalments on MODERNTHERM windows?
Yes. We provide a 5-year warranty on MODERNTHERM windows, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. We carry out the fitting with our own Pro-Plast crew with care for the seal and the finish, and as a local Białystok firm we provide aftercare. The survey and quote are free.
