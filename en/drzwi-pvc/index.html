Which PVC doors do we offer in Białystok?

Our PVC doors split into two distinct groups, because their jobs are completely different. The first is HS terrace and balcony doors — a large, effortlessly sliding glazing that opens the living room onto the terrace or garden. The second is MODERNDOOR entrance doors — warm, energy-efficient PVC exterior doors for the main or side entrance.

Both groups share what PVC is valued for: a tight seal, durability and minimal upkeep. The profiles need no painting or varnishing, they are easy to wipe down, and they cope well with rain, frost and temperature swings — of which there is no shortage near Białystok. Not sure which to choose? Start by deciding whether you need a way out onto the terrace (HS) or an entrance door (MODERNDOOR).

HS terrace doors versus MODERNDOOR entrance doors

These are two different products for two different places in the home. HS terrace doors (tilt-and-slide) on a Salamander profile with HS-Portal hardware are a solution for very large openings: up to 2.5 m high and 5 m wide, with a leaf up to 400 kg and a low, barrier-free threshold. Here glazing, light and easy access to the terrace matter most — you'll find the full description and parameters on the HS terrace doors page.

MODERNDOOR entrance doors (Sonarol), in turn, are exterior doors on the MODERNTHERM platform: 6-chamber profiles 80 mm deep, an aluminium threshold with a thermal break, a 3-point G-U Secury Automatic lock and very good insulation (Ud from 0,5 W/m²K with a 48 mm unit). These are doors for the entrance — warm, secure and representative. Details on the MODERNDOOR doors page.

  • Want to open the living room onto the terrace or garden? Choose HS.
  • Looking for warm doors for the main or side entrance? Choose MODERNDOOR.

Measurement, fitting and guarantee in Białystok

Frequently asked questions

What PVC doors can I order from Pro-Plast?
We offer two groups of PVC doors: HS terrace and balcony doors (a large, tilt-and-slide glazing on a Salamander profile) and MODERNDOOR entrance doors (Sonarol) on the MODERNTHERM platform. The first is a way out to the terrace, the second is a warm entrance door. We'll advise which suits your home.
HS terrace doors or MODERNDOOR entrance doors — how do they differ?
HS terrace doors are a large, effortlessly sliding leaf (up to 2.5 × 5 m) with a low threshold — for opening the living room onto the terrace. MODERNDOOR is a PVC exterior entrance door: 6-chamber profiles 80 mm, a warm aluminium threshold and a 3-point lock. HS is a way out to the terrace, MODERNDOOR is a door for the entrance.
Are PVC doors warm and energy-efficient?
Yes. We fit HS terrace doors with a 24–44 mm glazing unit (glass Ug = 1,1 W/m²K), while MODERNDOOR entrance doors reach Ud = 1,1 W/m²K (24 mm unit) or Ud = 0,5 W/m²K (48 mm unit) and qualify for the Czyste Powietrze programme.
Do you fit PVC doors in Białystok with a guarantee?
Yes. We fit with our own Pro-Plast crew in Białystok and the surrounding area, with a warm, tight threshold. We give a 5-year guarantee, offer instalments, and the on-site measurement and written quote are free. Book a free measurement via the contact page.
