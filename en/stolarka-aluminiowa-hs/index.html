HS ALU Terrace Doors in Białystok — a Panoramic Way Out to the Terrace

HS ALU is an aluminium lift-and-slide terrace door for very large glazing. A sash weighing up to 400 kg and measuring up to 3,2 × 3,3 m glides lightly, while a low threshold gives a comfortable way out to the terrace and garden.

HS ALU Doors — Key Specs

HS ALU terrace doors are aluminium lift-and-slide doors for the situation where a terrace is to join the living room through one vast glazed opening. The HS mechanism lifts the sash for sliding, so even a huge, heavy pane moves smoothly and lightly in a single move — and stays stable once lowered.

The sash can weigh up to 400 kg and reach dimensions of up to 3,2 m high and 3,3 m wide. It is a solution for very large openings that classic hinged doors can no longer handle. The low threshold makes crossing easier — a comfortable way out to the terrace, also for the elderly or with a pushchair. The structure is sealed with EPDM gaskets.

Why Aluminium in HS Doors?

The larger the pane of glass, the more weight the door structure has to carry — and this is where aluminium comes in. It is more rigid than uPVC, so with huge HS sashes it keeps its shape and guides them evenly, without sagging. As a result HS ALU handles dimensions that a uPVC profile, at such a weight, can no longer provide.

Aluminium also gives a slim frame — less profile, more glass and light — and durability: it does not warp in the sun and copes with Podlasie weather for years. If the terrace or balcony opening is smaller, HS doors on a PVC profile (HS Salamander) work just as well — often at a lower price. We will suggest which solution suits your terrace and budget.

Who Are HS ALU Doors For?

HS ALU doors are the answer for those who want a panoramic, low-threshold way out to the terrace or garden — with no compromise on the size of the glazing. They work well in homes and premises in Białystok and the surrounding area.

  • Large living-room-to-terrace glazing — when you want the living room wall to open onto the garden.
  • Very wide openings — sizes up to 3,2 × 3,3 m and a sash up to 400 kg, beyond the reach of classic hinged doors.
  • A comfortable way out to the terrace — a low threshold makes crossing easier, also for the elderly and with a pushchair.
  • A modern, slim look — a narrow aluminium frame lets in the maximum amount of light.

If maximum warmth or a lower price at a smaller opening matters to you, consider the PVC version. We will advise whether aluminium or HS doors on a PVC profile is the better fit. See also our full aluminium joinery range.

Frequently asked questions

How large can HS ALU terrace doors be?
Aluminium HS ALU terrace doors handle a sash weighing up to 400 kg and measuring up to 3,2 m high and 3,3 m wide. It is a solution for very large glazing that classic hinged doors can no longer handle. Despite the large pane, the sash lifts for sliding and glides lightly.
What are HS lift-and-slide doors?
HS (lift-and-slide) doors are terrace doors in which the mechanism lifts the sash for sliding and, once lowered, presses it firmly and seals it. As a result even a heavy, vast pane glides smoothly and lightly in a single move, and once closed it stays stable and airtight.
Do HS ALU doors have a low threshold?
Yes. HS ALU doors have a low threshold that makes crossing between the living room and the terrace easier. It is a comfortable, safe way out to the terrace or garden, also for the elderly and for those using a pushchair.
HS ALU or HS on a PVC profile — which to choose?
HS ALU is chosen for very large, heavy glazing (sash up to 400 kg, sizes up to 3,2 × 3,3 m), where the rigidity of aluminium and a slim frame are needed. For smaller terrace and balcony openings, HS doors on a PVC profile (HS Salamander) work just as well, often at a lower price. We will advise what suits your terrace and budget.

Yes.
