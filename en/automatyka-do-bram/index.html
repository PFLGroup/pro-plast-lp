Garage Door Automation in Białystok — SOMFY

You pull up to the garage, press the remote and don't get out of the car. For garage doors we choose SOMFY drives — a world leader in door automation — from the DEXXO range: OPTIMO, PRO 1000 and the smart SMART IO. Obstacle detection, an anti-burglary lock and a 5-year warranty as standard.

SOMFY DEXXO — Three Drives for Every Door

SOMFY is a world leader in the production of garage door automation. In our range we have three drives from the DEXXO series, chosen to suit the area and the intensity of use of the door:

≤ 10 m²DEXXO OPTIMO — 36,500 cycles
> 10 m²DEXXO PRO 1000 — 90,000 cycles
SMART IOsmart, LED lighting
5 yearswarranty

SOMFY DEXXO OPTIMO

A drive for doors up to 10 m², rated for 36,500 cycles. Ready to operate in less than 90 seconds after fitting. The rail comes in a chain or belt variant.

SOMFY DEXXO PRO 1000

A stronger drive for doors over 10 m², with the capacity for intensive work and up to 90,000 cycles. Fitted with an LCD display for programming, a partial-opening function, control of external lighting (up to 500 W) and an alarm siren signalling an attempt to force the door open. The rail comes with a chain, a belt or reinforced-durability tracks.

SOMFY DEXXO SMART IO

A smart drive for sectional garage doors, complete with rail and belt, with integrated zone LED lighting and independent control of interior lighting.

io-homecontrol, TaHoma and Connexoon Control

SOMFY drives communicate wirelessly using io-homecontrol radio technology, which you operate from the TaHoma® app, while you manage your devices through the CONNEXOON ACCESS app. You open the door with a remote, a button or from a smartphone, and the system notifies you of the door's status.

  • Obstacle detection — integrated, with sensitivity adjustment; the door stops when something gets in its way.
  • Partial-opening function — e.g. to air the garage.
  • External lighting control — up to 500 W in the PRO 1000.
  • Emergency battery backup and compatibility with solar power — the door works even in a power cut.

Safety and a 5-Year Warranty

A drive is not only about convenience but also protection. SOMFY fits the DEXXO with an anti-burglary lock, which locks the closed door and makes it hard to lift from outside. The PRO 1000 model additionally warns with an alarm siren on an attempt to force it open, and informs you of the door's status with a notification on your smartphone.

The manufacturer gives a 5-year warranty on the SOMFY DEXXO OPTIMO and PRO 1000 drives. This is the only element of the door range for which the warranty is stated explicitly — for the doors themselves (sectional, double-leaf and industrial) we settle the warranty terms individually when quoting.

Drive Selection and Fitting in Białystok

We match the drive to the area and weight of the door: for doors up to 10 m² the DEXXO OPTIMO is usually enough, for larger doors and more frequent use the PRO 1000 is better, and when you want control from your phone and LED lighting — the SMART IO. We check this at the free on-site survey.

We fit the automation together with a sectional door or add it to a door already fitted. After fitting we show you how to use the remote and the app and how to release the door in an emergency. We offer transport and sale by instalments. For the details get in touch or call: 602 488 413 or 85 746 14 46.

Frequently asked questions

Which SOMFY drive should I choose for my door?
The DEXXO OPTIMO is intended for doors up to 10 m² and rated for 36,500 cycles. The DEXXO PRO 1000 is a drive for doors over 10 m², capable of intensive work and up to 90,000 cycles, with an LCD display and an alarm siren. The DEXXO SMART IO is the smart variant with LED lighting, complete with rail and belt. We settle the choice at the survey.
How is a SOMFY drive controlled?
SOMFY drives work using wireless io-homecontrol radio technology. You open the door with a remote, a button or from a smartphone through the TaHoma app, while you manage your devices with the CONNEXOON ACCESS app. The system also informs you of the door's status with a notification on your phone.
Will the drive stop the door when something is in its way?
Yes. SOMFY DEXXO drives have integrated obstacle detection with sensitivity adjustment — the door stops when an obstacle appears in its way. This is a basic protection of people and property in everyday use.
Will a door with a drive work in a power cut?
Yes. SOMFY DEXXO drives support emergency battery backup and are compatible with solar power, so the door works even in a power cut. In addition you can always open the door by hand in an emergency — we show this method after fitting.
What warranty do SOMFY drives have?
The manufacturer gives a 5-year warranty on the SOMFY DEXXO OPTIMO and PRO 1000 drives. This is the only element of the door range with the warranty stated explicitly — for the doors themselves (sectional, double-leaf, industrial) we settle the warranty terms individually when quoting.
