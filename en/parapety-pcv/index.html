uPVC Window Sills in Białystok — Internal

An internal uPVC sill is the most popular and most economical choice for flats and houses. A high-impact core, veneer lamination and a smooth surface give a sill that looks good and is easy to clean for years.

What Is a uPVC Sill Made Of?

An internal uPVC sill is the most popular choice for interiors — economical, practical and resistant to everyday use. The core is made of high-impact polyvinyl chloride and laminated with high-quality uPVC veneers, so the surface is smooth, uniform and easy to keep clean.

The material resists moisture, staining and typical cleaning agents, so the sill works well even where it is wiped down regularly. It is a solution that looks good from day one and needs no special care. In Białystok and across Podlasie, uPVC sills are our most frequently ordered internal option.

  • High-impact uPVC core, laminated with uPVC veneers.
  • Smooth, uniform surface, easy to clean.
  • Resistant to moisture, staining and cleaning agents.
  • The most popular, economical choice for interiors.

What Colours and Finishes Are Available?

You can match a uPVC sill to the colour of the window and the whole interior. There is a wide range of veneers to choose from — from classic white to wood-effect decors that warm up a room and go with floors or furniture.

We cut the sill to any length, and the end caps come in the colour of the sill, so the whole thing looks consistent. During transport and fitting the surface is protected with a film. uPVC sills are compatible with uPVC, timber and aluminium windows, so we'll match them to any joinery — including the uPVC windows in our range.

  • Wide range of veneers: white and wood-effect decors.
  • Any length, end caps in the colour of the sill.
  • Protective film during transport and fitting.
  • For uPVC, timber and aluminium windows.

Choosing and Fitting a uPVC Sill

Choosing a uPVC sill starts with a free on-site survey. We check the depth of the recess, the width of the window and what the sill has to cope with day to day — only then do we talk about colour and overhang. A sill is best planned together with replacing the joinery, because then the dressings and fitting come out clean, with no touch-ups.

We carry out the fitting with our own Pro-Plast crew, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. As a local Białystok firm we give a 5-year warranty. We're glad to combine a uPVC sill with new uPVC windows — book a free survey or call 602 488 413. You'll find the other types of sill on the window sills page.

Frequently asked questions

What are uPVC sills made of?
Internal uPVC sills have a core of high-impact polyvinyl chloride, laminated with high-quality uPVC veneers. As a result the surface is smooth, uniform and easy to keep clean, and the sill resists moisture and typical cleaning agents.
Is a uPVC sill easy to maintain?
Yes. The smooth, laminated surface does not absorb dirt, resists moisture and staining, and ordinary cleaning agents are enough. This is one of the main advantages of uPVC sills and the reason they are the most popular choice for interiors.
What colours of uPVC sill are available?
There is a wide range of veneers to choose from — from classic white to wood-effect decors. We'll match the sill to the colour of the window and the interior, and the end caps come in the colour of the sill. We'll show the exact patterns during the free survey.
Does a uPVC sill suit timber and aluminium windows?
Yes. uPVC sills are compatible with uPVC, timber and aluminium windows, so we'll match them to any joinery. Most often we combine them with our uPVC windows, but we'll fit them to windows you already have as well.
How much does a uPVC sill cost in Białystok?
The price depends on the dimensions, the veneer colour and the finish. uPVC sills are the most economical internal option. After a free survey we'll give a specific written quote, with no obligation, and the purchase can be spread over instalments.
