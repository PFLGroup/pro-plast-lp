redENERGY uPVC Windows in Białystok — Warmth and Roto Hardware

redENERGY is a warm, triple-glazed uPVC window line at Uw 0,8 W/m²K with Roto hardware. You choose between two design variants — redTECHENERGY and redARTEENERGY — both with a warm-edge spacer as standard and a 5-year warranty.

redENERGY at a glance — key specs

redENERGY is for people who want very good insulation and branded hardware without overpaying for passive-house parameters. The whole set — profile, triple-glazed unit and Roto hardware — works toward one goal: a warm home and a lower heating bill.

The Uw = 0,8 W/m²K value applies to the whole window, not just the glass — a real level for a warm, energy-efficient home. The unit is triple-glazed, and you can optionally choose glass at U = 0,5 or U = 0,6 W/m²K for even better insulation. The profile is reinforced with 2 mm steel, and a warm-edge spacer comes as standard, cutting the thermal bridge at the glass edge and reducing the risk of condensation in winter.

Two design variants — redTECHENERGY and redARTEENERGY

redENERGY comes in two variants that differ mainly in the look of the sash, not in the thermal class. This lets you match the window to the character of your façade without giving up the Uw 0,8 W/m²K value.

How do redTECHENERGY and redARTEENERGY differ?

  • redTECHENERGY — a classic, clean profile for most houses and flats.
  • redARTEENERGY — a wider 84 mm sash and a more substantial, designer profile line.
  • Both variants share the same base: Uw 0,8 W/m²K, a triple-glazed unit, 2 mm steel reinforcement and a warm-edge spacer as standard.

In practice the choice comes down to aesthetics: if you want a bolder, fuller sash look, go for redARTEENERGY. If you prefer a neutral, universal profile, redTECHENERGY is the natural pick. During the survey we'll show you both and advise which suits your façade better.

Roto hardware and security

Everyday comfort and security come down to the hardware, which is why the redENERGY line runs on branded Roto systems. We fit Roto NT hardware with a RotoSil Nano coating that increases corrosion resistance — this matters especially in the humid Podlasie climate, where a window works under large temperature swings for half the year.

As standard we fit a Roto Secustic handle — anti-burglary, making it harder to lever or shift the sash from the outside. That is a real obstacle for a burglar, not just a marketing line, so redENERGY also works well in windows facing the garden or on the ground floor.

Who redENERGY is for and how to order in Białystok

redENERGY is a sensible choice for detached houses and flats in Białystok and across Podlasie where you want very good insulation and branded Roto hardware, but not necessarily the top, premium tier. It's the "golden middle" line — warm, secure and with a 5-year warranty.

How do the redTECHENERGY and redARTEENERGY variants differ?
Both share the same thermal base (Uw 0,8 W/m²K, triple glazing, 2 mm steel reinforcement and a warm-edge spacer) and differ in design. redARTEENERGY has a wider 84 mm sash and a more substantial, designer profile, while redTECHENERGY is the classic, universal variant.
