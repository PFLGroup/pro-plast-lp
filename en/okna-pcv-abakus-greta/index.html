ABAKUS bluENERGY GRETA PVC Windows — design inspired by concrete

GRETA are windows with an industrial design inspired by raw concrete, made of 100% recycled material. Salamander bluEvolution 82, Uw 0,76 W/m²K and installation by Pro-Plast in Białystok.

What makes bluENERGY GRETA windows special?

GRETA are windows with an industrial design inspired by raw concrete — the Greta colour is a concrete shade of grey with a slightly rough, natural texture. That is its main distinguishing feature versus smooth foils.

The second distinguishing feature is ecology: the GRETA profile is made of 100% recycled material. The construction is based on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 platform — 6-chamber, reinforced profiles with an 82 mm build depth.

What warmth and security does GRETA provide?

GRETA reaches Uw 0,76 W/m²K with a triple-glazed, 2-chamber unit at Ug 0,5 W/m²K. Despite its raw, concrete character, it is a fully energy-efficient window, well suited to Podlasie winters.

The sealing consists of two colour-matched stop gaskets and one central gasket, welded at the corners. Security is provided by Roto NX hardware, the anti-burglary Roto Line Secustik handle and burglar resistance up to the RC2 class.

  • Triple-glazed, 2-chamber unit, Ug 0,5 W/m²K
  • 2 colour-matched stop gaskets + 1 central gasket
  • 2 mm steel reinforcements and a warm edge spacer between the panes
  • IRMC Production® welding technology

What do the colours and finish of GRETA look like?

GRETA comes in one strong colour character — the Greta shade inspired by concrete, with a slightly rough texture. It is a solution for interiors and façades in an industrial, loft and modern style.

The anti-burglary Roto Line Secustik handle can be chosen in the colours:

  • Silver (R01.1)
  • Matt Titanium (R01.3)

You can see the other models in the PVC windows section. If you want to complete the industrial look of the window zone, we will also match suitable window sills — arrange the details via contact with the office in Białystok.
