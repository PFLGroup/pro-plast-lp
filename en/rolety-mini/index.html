MINI Roller Blinds in Białystok — Compact Blinds for Small Windows

MINI is a compact, free-hanging fabric roller blind with a smaller cassette and roller tube. A self-braking mechanism, fitting to the reveal, wall or ceiling and 11 fitting colours — ideal for smaller windows and tight recesses.

MINI — A Compact Free-Hanging Blind

MINI is the compact version of the free-hanging blind — with a smaller cassette and roller tube than the STANDARD version. Like STANDARD it is free-hanging and self-braking, but thanks to its compact dimensions it fits where a larger blind would be too big. It is a solution designed for smaller windows and tight recesses.

The self-braking mechanism stops the blind exactly where you set it, with no cords to tie. The smaller cassette and roller tube make MINI discreet and keep it from taking up room in a shallow recess — and it is just as simple to use as STANDARD.

Fitting and 11 Fitting Colours

MINI, like STANDARD, is fitted in three ways, and the fittings — the guide rails and the roller tube — can be chosen in 11 colours, so the compact blind blends into the window and the interior.

  • Fitting to the reveal — the compact cassette fits even in a shallow recess.
  • Fitting to the wall — above the window, when the recess is shallow or you want to cover more.
  • Fitting to the ceiling — for unusual window arrangements and deep recesses.
  • 11 fitting colours — match the guide rails and roller tube to the colour of the window or the interior.

During the survey we will check the dimensions of the window and the recess and suggest whether MINI or a larger blind will work better — and show you fabric samples and the fitting colours.

Who Is MINI For?

MINI is the choice for windows and recesses where every centimetre counts — when you want a compact, discreet blind that will not overwhelm a small window. It works well in bathrooms, kitchens and small windows in homes and flats in Białystok and the surrounding area.

  • Smaller windows — the compact cassette fits where a large blind would be too heavy visually.
  • Tight recesses — the smaller roller tube fits in a shallow reveal.
  • A discreet look — the compact cassette does not take up room and does not catch the eye.
  • Convenience and matching — a self-braking mechanism and 11 fitting colours.

Do you have a larger, typical window? Consider the versatile STANDARD version. Would you rather control the light with slats — check out venetian blinds, and protection against insects is provided by insect screens. Frequently asked questions

What makes the MINI blind stand out?
MINI is a compact free-hanging fabric roller blind with a smaller cassette and roller tube than STANDARD. It is self-braking, fitted to the reveal, wall or ceiling, and the fittings come in 11 colours. Thanks to its compact dimensions, it works well for smaller windows and in tight recesses.
How does MINI differ from the STANDARD blind?
Both blinds are free-hanging, self-braking, fitted to the reveal, wall or ceiling, and they come with 11 fitting colours. MINI has a smaller, compact cassette and roller tube, which is why we recommend it for smaller windows and tight recesses. STANDARD is the versatile version for typical windows.
What windows does the MINI blind suit?
MINI is designed for smaller windows and tight recesses where a larger blind would be too big or would not fit in a shallow reveal. It works well in bathrooms, kitchens and at small windows, among others. During the survey we will check the dimensions and suggest the best version.
How is the MINI blind fixed?
MINI can be fixed to the reveal, the wall or the ceiling — just like STANDARD. The compact cassette fits even in a shallow recess. During the survey we assess the window and the recess and suggest the best way of fitting.
Do you fit MINI blinds in Białystok?
