ABAKUS goGRAIN Windows in Białystok — Bio-Hybrid with Spelt

goGRAIN is a bio-hybrid uPVC window from ABAKUS on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 platform, with 10% natural spelt husk in the profile. Uw 0,76 W/m²K, an 82 mm depth and Roto NX hardware give warmth, RC2 class and a markedly lower carbon footprint.

goGRAIN — What Window Is It and What Are Its Specs?

goGRAIN is a bio-hybrid uPVC window in which 10% of the profile material is natural spelt husk — and that is what sets it apart from classic uPVC. It is built on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 platform with an 82 mm build depth, 6-chamber frame and sash profiles, and reaches a whole-window value of Uw = 0,76 W/m²K.

Warmth comes from a triple-glazed, twin-chamber unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K and an arrangement of three gaskets: two colour-matched abutment gaskets and one central one, welded at the corners. It is a window for those who want to combine good insulation with a lower environmental impact.

Why Does 10% Spelt Husk Make a Difference?

Adding 10% natural spelt husk is not marketing but a real change in the profile's properties. Replacing part of the plastic with a plant-based raw material cuts the CO₂ emissions generated during profile production by up to 20%.

  • CO₂ emissions cut by up to 20% — a lower carbon footprint for the window already at the profile production stage.
  • Linear expansion lower by 30% — the profile "works" less under temperature changes, which favours the window's durability and geometry.
  • Shrinkage lower by 15% — greater dimensional stability of the profile over time.
  • A unique bio-hybrid surface — a natural texture that classic, smooth uPVC cannot give.

The profile is further strengthened by stiffening ribs and a 2 mm steel reinforcement, and we join the corners using the seamless Cutmaster technology. If you are comparing lines for eco-credentials, set goGRAIN against the rest of the uPVC windows in our range.

Security and Hardware — How Does goGRAIN Protect?

goGRAIN combines eco-design with security at RC2 class. Burglary resistance is handled by Roto NX perimeter hardware and a Roto Line Secustik anti-burglary handle or lumiNO handles.

  • RC2 class — makes it harder to lever the window open with simple tools, a sensible standard for ground floors and easily reachable windows.
  • Roto NX hardware — a durable, smoothly operating tilt-and-turn mechanism.
  • Roto Line Secustik handle — blocks the handle from being turned from outside; alternatively, the elegant lumiNO handles.
  • 2 mm steel reinforcement — a rigid base for the hardware and the whole structure.

It is a good solution for detached houses in Podlasie, where both warmth and a sense of security matter. During the survey we will pick the handle and the scope of security to suit your windows.

Fitting in Białystok, Warranty and Instalments

Even a bio-hybrid window at Uw 0,76 will not deliver its full benefits with a poor fit — skipped insulation where it meets the wall spoils the result despite good specs. That is why we fit goGRAIN with our own Pro-Plast crew, with a warm, airtight fit and a careful finish; we weld the profiles using Abakus IRMC Production technology.

We start with a free on-site survey and a free written quote, we provide transport, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. As a local Białystok firm we give a 5-year warranty and aftercare. Book a free survey and find out the goGRAIN window price for your home.

Frequently asked questions

How does goGRAIN differ from ordinary uPVC windows?
goGRAIN is a bio-hybrid — 10% of the profile material is natural spelt husk, which cuts CO₂ emissions in production by up to 20% and reduces linear expansion by 30% and shrinkage by 15%. It is built on the Salamander bluEvolution 82 platform and reaches Uw = 0,76 W/m²K.
What Uw value do goGRAIN windows have?
goGRAIN windows reach a Uw = 0,76 W/m²K. It is provided by the 6-chamber Salamander bluEvolution 82 profile with an 82 mm depth combined with a triple-glazed, twin-chamber unit at Ug = 0,5 W/m²K.
Is goGRAIN an eco-friendly window?
Yes. goGRAIN contains 10% natural spelt husk in the profile, which cuts the CO₂ emissions of profile production by up to 20%. It is a bio-hybrid window for those who want to combine good thermal insulation with a lower carbon footprint.
What burglary-resistance class does goGRAIN have?
goGRAIN is available in RC2 class. Security is handled by Roto NX hardware and a Roto Line Secustik anti-burglary handle (or lumiNO handles), while a 2 mm steel reinforcement adds rigidity.

Yes. We provide a 5-year warranty on goGRAIN windows, and the purchase can be spread over instalments. We fit them with our own Pro-Plast crew in Białystok, with a warm, airtight fit. Survey, quote and transport are part of our offer, and the survey and quote are free.
